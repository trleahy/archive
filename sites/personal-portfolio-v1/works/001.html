001 : Bexhill After Dark

An annual light festival with an illuminated parade and creative light shows. In association with 18 Hours.

I was approached by 18 Hours Events, who was keen to work with young people and community groups as part of the project based around the De La Warr Pavilion, Bexhill.
The final product was to be used as a promotion of the piece for Circo Rum Baba and 18 Hours Events.
